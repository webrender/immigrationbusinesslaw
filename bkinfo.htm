Background Information
        I graduated from the University of Miami, Coral Gables, Florida, being awarded a Bachelor of Arts Degree, magna cum laude, with combined majors in History and Geography. Subsequently thereto, I attended Northwestern University, Evanston, Illinois, pursuant to a Northwestern University Fellowship and was awarded a Master of Arts Degree in Political Geography. Thereafter, I attended the University of Miami, Coral Gables, Florida and was awarded my J.D. Degree. I am currently a member in good standing of the following State Bars: California and Florida. Under the Specialist Program of the California State Bar, I am and have been a Certified Immigration and Nationality Law Specialist since 1992 to date.
